Eraserheads is a Filipino band formed in 1989 and they are the most influential and successful in the history of Filipino music as they sold 9 million albums throughout their career. They are considered as “The Beatles of the Philippines”. The band released several singles, albums, and EPs and achieved commercial success with their third album Cutterpillow in 1995, which achieved platinum status several times. They also received several awards including Viewer's Choice Award at the 1997 MTV Video Music Awards in New York, the only Filipino artist to have received the award before the conception of the MTV Asia Awards. The band consists of 4 members and they are Ely Buendia, Buddy Zabala, Raymund Marasigan, and Marcus Adoro.
